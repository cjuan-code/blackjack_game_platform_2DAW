Rounds:
  • Bet round: in this round, player bets an amount of coins.
  • Dealing round: in this round, cards are dealed. Player gets 2 cards which are visible, crupier gets 2 cards too, but the second one is not visible.
  • Playing round: in this round, player can:
    • Hit: it draws a card to player.
    • Stand: if player thinks that has enought points to win, player can stand and pass the turn to crupier.
Scoring:
  • AS: equals 1 or 11 points.
  • Jack, Queen, King: equals 10 points.
  • Other cards values equals to its number.

Winning prices:
  • If player has more points than crupier. Winnings are multiplied by 1.
  • If player get Blackjack (21 points at dealing round). Winnings are multipied by 1.5
  • If player has less points than crupier. Bet amount is lost.

Anyway, when player wins, refunds bet quantity plus winnings.

Chips:
  • Orange: 5 coins
  • Red: 10 coins
  • Green: 25 coins
  • Blue: 50 coins
  • Yellow: 100 coins
