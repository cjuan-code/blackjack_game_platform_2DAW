Rounds:
  • Bet round: in this round, player bets an amount of coins.
  • Drawing round: in this round, cards are drawed. Player gets 2 cards which are visible, crupier gets 2 cards too, but the second one is not visible. There can be different situations:
    • If crupier's first card is an AS, player can bet that crupier has Blackjack, so player can refund an amount of coins that has been bet.
    • In case that is not an AS:
      • Hit: it draws a card to player.
      • Stand: if player thinks that has enought points to win, player can stand and pass the turn to crupier.
Scoring:
  • AS: equals 1 or 11 points.
  • Jack, Queen, King: equals 10 points.
  • Other cards values equals to its number.

Winning prices:
  • If player has more points than crupier. Winning is multiplied by 1.
  • If player get Blackjack (21 points when drawing). Winning is multipied by 1.5
  • If player has less points than crupier. Bet amount is lost.

Anyway, when player wins, refunds bet quantity plus winnings
